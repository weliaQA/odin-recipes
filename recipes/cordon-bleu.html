Chicken Cordon Bleu Casserole

Description:

A simpler and cleaner method of making Chicken Cordon Bleu. This casserole method simplifies the process and makes individual portions much easier!

Ingredients:

Crumb Topping:

    • 1/3 cup panko breadcrumbs
      2 tablespoons unsalted butter
      1 tablespoon finely grated parmesean cheese
      1 pinch freshly ground black pepper

    • Chicken:

    • 1 large boneless skinless chicken breast
      1/8 teaspoon salt
      1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
      1 pinch cayenne pepper
      1 pinch fresh thyme
      2 tablespoons of heavy cream
      1 rounded teaspoon of dijon mustard
      1 oz diced smoked ham
      1 oz grated gruyere cheese

    • Steps:

    1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F - 220 degrees C
    2. Place panko in bowl, add butter, parmesean and black pepper, stir until mixure resembles wet sand
    3. Cut chicken into 1/2 inch cubes and transfer to a bowl. Season with sat, black pepper, cayenne, thyme, and salt. Add cream, dijon, ham, and 2/3 of the gruyere
    4. Stir chicken mixture to combine, transfer into small baking dish and top with the remainder of the gruyere
    5. Spoon breadcrumb mixture over the top, poke crumbs down lightly into the chicken with the tip of the spoon before evening out the top
    6. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is cooked through, the casserole is bubbling around the edges, and the top is browned, about 25 minutes. Let rest for 5 minutes
