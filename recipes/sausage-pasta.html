Creamy Sausage Pasta

Description:

A pasta dish containing sausage in a creamy sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 large garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pound mild italian sausage
  • 5 fl oz of white wine
  • 5 fl oz of heavy whipping cream
  • 8 oz of penne or rigatoni
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup of grated parmesean cheese
  • fresh parsley

Steps:

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water, cook pasta until tender, reserve 1 cup of pasta water for later
  2. Cook sausage in a skillet over medium heat, start with olive oil, add sausage and break up with a spatlia until it begins to brown
  3. Add garlic and oil if needed, pour in wine and scrape any brown bits off of the bottom of the skillet
  4. Simmer until wine has reduced by about half, add pasta water and half of the parmesean, stir then add cream. Stir again, season with salt and pepper
  5. Stir in pasta, check for seasoning, then add remaining parmesean, garnish with parsley and serve
