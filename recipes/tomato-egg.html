Tomato And Egg Stirfry

Description:

A traditional Chinese Dish, combines tomato and egg and is usually served over rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 medium tomatoes, cut into 1-inch wedges
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet at medium-high heat, add green onions, saute for 15-30 seconds, then add beaten eggs
  2. Cook until eggs are nearly set, transfer eggs into a bowl
  3. Add tomatoes to pan, saute, stirring occasionally, until tomatoes are slightly wilted and releasing juices, add salt and sugar, mix well
  4. Add the eggs back to the skillet, stirring ocassionally for 2 to 3 minutes
